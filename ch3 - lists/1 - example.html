Tea Recipie

How to Make Best TEA Recipe at Home easily

Ingradients:

  • Milk
  • Lipton
  • Sugar
  • Kettle
  • Water

Method:

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Insert lipton on water
  3. Insert Sugar on water.
  4. Mix well and boil 1 to 2 minute
  5. Insert Milk and boil 4 to 5 minutes
  6. Now your Tea is Ready.
